Our Voyage

Yachts Zenith, birthed in London's dynamic core by the Thames, embarked on a mission to transform luxury yachting. Julian Pirvu, fuelled by his profound sea love, launched more than a charter service; he created a portal to the splendour of yachting for all. With a focus on excellence and discretion, Julian handpicked a fleet of elegant, high-performance yachts, chosen as much for their beauty as for the remarkable experiences they could offer. Each Zenith voyage, whether a quaint gathering or a grand festivity, is tailored to its guests, embodying a philosophy that yachting is an exciting, inclusive journey. Gaining renown for outstanding service and privacy, Yachts Zenith drew a worldwide clientele, attracted to its tailored experiences and unwavering discretion.
