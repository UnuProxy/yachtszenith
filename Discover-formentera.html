Discover Formentera with Yachts Zenith Ibiza

Embark on an Unforgettable Journey

Formentera, a gem in the Mediterranean, stands as one of Europe's most pristine and untouched islands. It’s a paradise that seems almost too good to be true. A favored destination for our charter clients is Illetes, the closest point of Formentera to Ibiza, known for its serene beauty and crystal-clear waters.

But Illetes is just the beginning. To truly immerse yourself in the Formentera experience, we at Zenith Yachts encourage you to explore beyond. Set sail to other enchanting beaches on the island, such as Cala Saona, Es Calo, and Es Migjorn, each offering its own unique charm and tranquility.

Playa Illetes in Formentera is a pristine yachting paradise, renowned for its crystal-clear waters and powdery white sands. This tranquil beach, set in a UNESCO-listed park, offers serene sunbathing, swimming, and stunning views of Ibiza.

Es Palmador is a small, idyllic island located just north of Formentera, celebrated for its unspoiled, crystal-clear waters and tranquil environment

Cala Saona in Formentera is a picturesque cove known for its tranquil turquoise waters and soft sandy shores. Nestled between rocky cliffs, it offers a serene and intimate beach experience.

Es Migjorn in Formentera is a long, serene stretch of beach known for its unspoiled beauty and tranquil atmosphere. With crystal-clear waters and fine golden sands, it offers a perfect setting for relaxation and swimming

Es Pujols in Formentera is a vibrant resort town, known for its lively beach scene, diverse dining, and nightlife.
